To measure power data during a ride:
  1. From the Home page, press the RECORD button
  2. Press the PAUSE button to pause recording
  3. Press SAVE to end recording
  4. The App will then jump to the Summary page, which shows ride summary data such as average power

    Other tips

    1. Use the Setup page to fine tune data for estimating your power output
    2. After the ride, use the web app at www.BikePowerGauge.org to plot and analyze your ride data
    3. Also see the web app for directions for using a coast down test to measure your own drag coefficient
